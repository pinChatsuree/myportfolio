world chess re-design |

World Chess Official FIDE Broadcasting is the site as its main purpose is to broadcast competitions and provide a lot of candidates's information while also link to online arena, ratings, and shop.

UI/UX > World Chess - Official FIDE Broadcasting

What overall problems do they have?

From the landing moment to the site, let's take a look at what they lead users into.

  • No navigation bar
  • In the hero section, it is hard to start focusing on the provided information
  • Sections are not well-organised
  • CTAs are not consistent
  • Link and normal text looks the same
  • Some sections are duplicated
  • Footer isn't located in every page

Lack of indication: For example, once the 'watch live' button has been clicked, there is nothing inside this page. Instead, they should provide information before clicked that there is no current live available at the moment or provide schedul for it. Besides, there is no indication where can or cannot be clicked as a link or not so this may confuse users a lot.

Consistency doesn't exist: There is no consistent on all elements, such as headlines, links, buttons, hover effect, shadows, or even text of each content

Overall looks modern and simple yet there are plenty of user experience that are still being missed.

Keep goal-oriented in mind.

- Users able to click live session within one click
- Users be able to navigate from Home to Contact us page easily
- News section with easy access and noticeable and possibility to go back to homepage
- Make UI consistent throughout the sites
- To have more email subscribers
